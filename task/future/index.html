Types of doors

S-Type M-Type L-Type Q-Type V-Type

S-Type garage doors

S-Type garage doors are produced by S-type panels which are called “rib” and are the most universal and popular that suitable for different houses styles. S-Type garage doors are the most optimal choice of “price-quality”.

M-Type garage doors

These garage doors are produced on M-type panels basis and is an interesting alterantive for S-Type garage doors. M-Type garage doors is suitable for all houses styles such as modern, classic or minimalism ones.

L-Type garage doors

L-Type garage doors are produced by the whole panels without any stripes and maybe are the most elegant garage doors type. It is excellent suitable for the modern designer styles such as minimalism, modern or California. It is well combined with anthracite color.

Q-Type garage doors

Q-Type garage doors are produced by cassette panels and are the most traditional garage doors type.Q-Type garage doors are for people who appreciate the beauty and the charm of tradition. It is excellent suitable for houses in the American classic or Victorian styles.

V-Type garage doors

V-Type garage doors are produced by “microwave” panels and it is the innovative designer solution on the market.V-Type garage doors are the ideal solution in order to full fill the modern designer ideas and excellent suits for modern and minimalism styles.
